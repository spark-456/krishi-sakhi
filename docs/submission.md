---

## 1) Team Details (Slide 1)
Fill in:
- **Team name**
- **Team leader name**
- **Problem Statement** (1–2 lines; clear and measurable)

---

## 2) Brief About the Idea (Slide 2)
Write a short overview:
- What you’re building (one sentence)
- Who it is for (target users)
- What pain/problem it solves
- Why now (why it matters)



---

## 3) Opportunities (Slide 3)
You must address these questions:
- **How different is it** from existing ideas/solutions?
- **How will it solve** the problem?
- **USP** (Unique Selling Proposition) of the proposed solution



---

## 4) List of Features Offered by the Solution (Slide 4)
Provide a bullet list of core features:
- MVP features (must-have)
- Differentiators (nice-to-have)
- Safety/compliance features (if relevant)



---

## 5) Process Flow Diagram or Use-Case Diagram (Slide 5)
Add one of:
- A **process flow** (step-by-step user + system flow), OR
- A **use-case diagram** (actors + interactions)



---

## 6) Wireframes / Mock Diagrams (Optional) (Slide 6)
Optional but recommended:
- UI wireframes (low/high fidelity)
- Screen flow (home → key actions → results)
- Admin/monitoring views (if applicable)



---

## 7) Architecture Diagram of the Proposed Solution (Slide 7)
Include:
- Frontend, backend, DB/storage
- AI/ML components (if any)
- Integrations/APIs
- Auth/security boundary (where auth happens, where secrets live)



---

## 8) Technologies to Be Used in the Solution (Slide 8)
List the tech stack clearly:
- Frontend
- Backend
- Database
- Cloud/hosting
- AI/ML frameworks (if any)
- DevOps/CI/CD (if any)



---

## 9) Usage of AMD Products / Solutions (Slide 9)
Explain how you are using AMD products/solutions in your build:
- Hardware/software usage (what + where)
- Why AMD is a fit (performance, efficiency, etc.)
- Any AMD-specific advantage you’re leveraging



---

## 10) Estimated Implementation Cost (Optional) (Slide 10)
Optional: Provide a rough estimate:
- Infra costs (monthly)
- Tooling/licensing
- Data acquisition (if any)
- Compute/training (if applicable)



---

## 11) Prototype Assets (Optional) (Slide 11)
Optional but recommended to include:
- **GitHub public repository link**
- **Demo video link** (**Max: 3 minutes**)



---

## 12) Add as Per the Requirements of the Contest (Slide 12)
A flexible slide to add anything extra the contest expects, such as:
- Impact / SDG alignment
- Unique metrics/results
- Validation/testing approach
- Roadmap/next steps
- Risks & mitigations
